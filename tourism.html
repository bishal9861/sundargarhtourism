Tourist Attraction

Khandadhar Waterfalls4.2/5

Amidst thick jungles, Khandadhar is a glittering waterfall created by a perennial rivulet called Korapani Nala within a few kms. of its origin. 244 meters in height it is said to be the highest waterfall in Odisha. 19kms.

Best Time To Visit:-June-December

Vedavyas Dham4.7/5

Situated on the confluence of the river Sankha and Koel, which thereafter from the river Brahmani, Vedavyas is not only a place of natural beauty but also is considered a Tritha a place of religious sanctity.

Best Time To Visit:-December-August

Vaishnodevi Temple3.9/5

Vaishnodevi Temple at Rourkela is a replica of the original Temple of Vaishnodevi of Jammu (Trikuta Hills) and is situated on the top of the Durgapur Hill facing towards Rourkela Steel Plant.

Best Time To Visit:-August-September

Mandira Dam4.5/5

Mandira dam is located near Kansbahal in Sundergarh district, Odisha, India. It is built across Sankh river, located 16 km upstream from Mandira. The water from the dam is used for Rourkela Steel Plant.

Best Time To Visit:-August-December

Bholenath Temple4.1/5

A dip in the confluence is belied to wash away sins. Tradition has it that Vyasadeva, the reputed sage, lived here and the great epic Mahabharat. There are three temples here, two dedicated to Lord Siva and one to Rama.

Best Time To Visit:-October-April

Hanuman Vatika3.8/5

Hanuman Vatika named after one of the highest statue of Hanuman. Inaugurated in the year 1994 by the Minister of Orissa, Biju Patnaik. Here a statue of Hanuman is constructed of height of 75 feet.

Best Time To Visit:-October-March
